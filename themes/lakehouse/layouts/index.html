The Lakehouse Approach

Not a school, not a tutor — a real mentorship that fits your family

01 Family First

I believe the best support starts with a strong partnership with the parents. I’d love to begin with a casual meet-and-greet consultation at your home or a local coffee shop where we will chat and see if we’re a good fit. Getting to know the family first allows us to build a foundation of trust and transparency before I begin working with your child.

02 Professional guidance

If we all agree it’s a good match, we move forward with our first session. The transition is designed to feel comfortable and purposeful as I begin my role as a mentor with specialized expertise in technology and practical execution. My goal is to build a professional rapport where your child feels motivated and ready to learn.

03 Real-World Immersion

Time at the lakehouse is filled with projects, tinkering, lake activities, and conversations. Instead of artificial entertainment, we focus on genuine experiences and practical problem-solving within a secure, supportive environment.

04 Lasting Impact

Progress reports, ongoing support, and a mentor they can always call. The goal is to spark curiosity and ignite love for life that carries through years, not just weekends.

05 Transparency & Trust

Your family’s peace of mind is my priority. Maintaining an open, transparent environment ensures everyone feels safe and confident, allowing us to focus entirely on your child’s growth and technical development.
